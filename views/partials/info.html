[TheSeeder] bot-syntax

  • Syntax for adding your magnets: ?add magnet YOUR_MAGNET
    Example: ?add magnet magnet:?xt=urn:btih:DF49AC16CF9668B4DDF8A559D69A6B2D58568C9F&dn=lmgtfy+1+1+ipa&tr=udp%3A%2F%2Ftracker.openbittorrent.com%3A80%2Fannounce&tr=udp%3A%2F%2Fopen.demonii.com%3A1337

    Example: ?status magnet DF49AC16CF9668B4DDF8A559D69A6B2D58568C9F

  • Syntax for displaying this syntax via PM: ?help
