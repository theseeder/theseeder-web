How To's related to the Self Service:

how to add new series

  • go to sickrage and log in
  • click on "Shows"->"Add Shows" and select "Add New Show"
  • enter the show name and select "Search" (the language only affects the search language so don't change it)
  • select the correct show you searched for and click "Next"
  • On Page 2 of 3 don't select anything and click "Next"
  • On the next Page select the Quality (preselect is: download 720p or better but replace with 1080p or better if found) and if you want the previously aired episodes to get snatched too select "Wanted" for "Status for previously aired episodes"
  • If your show is anime be sure to select "Anime"
  • Next click on "Add Show" to complete the process

how to add movies

  • go to couchpotato and log in
  • enter the movie name in the search box and select the movie you want to add
  • click on "Add" to complete the process

how to add .torrents

  • go to torrent and log in
  • click on the symbol top left in the corner which says "Add Torrent"
  • click "Browse" and select your .torrent
  • click "OK" to complete the process

how to add magnets

  • go to torrent and log in
  • click on the second symbol top left in the corner which says "Add Torrent from URL"
  • enter the magnet link into the "Torrent URL" field and select "OK" to complete the process

how to add links

  • go to jd and log in
  • click on "JD@THESEEDER" and go to "LINKCOLLECTOR"
  • click on "Add links" and enter the link(s) you want to download
  • if your archive is password protected enter it below
  • if your link is password protected enter it below
  • you might need to solve a captcha depending on the type of link you entered
  • if every part is online from at least one hoster right click on the package and select "Add to downloads" to complete the process

how to add dlc

  • go to jd and log in
  • click on "JD@THESEEDER" and go to "LINKCOLLECTOR"
  • click on the Dropdown to the right of "Add links" and select "Add Container"
  • add the dlc(s) and click "Upload"
  • if every part is online from at least one hoster right click on the package and select "Add to downloads" to complete the process
  • HINT: if your archive is password protected go to "SETTINGS" and enter it in "Add to Archive Password List" and click "Save"

how to find torrents

  • you can search for torrents on thepiratebay or use google

how to contribute to scripts

  • open git and fork the repo, create an account if you dont have one already
  • commit your changes to your repo
  • create a pull request on the original repo for yours to get merged
  • alternatively send me your changes via pm/email
